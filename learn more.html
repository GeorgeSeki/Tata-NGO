Our Mission

Think and Act for Tanzania (TATA) is devoted to improving the livelihoods of people with disabilities and other vulnerable groups through programs in Education, Health, Agriculture, Good Governance, and Road Safety.

About Our Work

Since our inception in August 2019, TATA has undertaken numerous grassroots activities based on community contributions:

  • Community awareness raising for people with disabilities
  • Voter education campaigns in Korogwe District, Tanga
  • Environmental protection and sanitation initiatives in Korogwe District (Manundu, Hale, Makinyumbi–Miembeni)
  • Data collection and needs assessment
  • Individual self-assessments and targeted support
