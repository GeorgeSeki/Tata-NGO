Under the current leadership, the organization is supported by sectoral committees in Education, Health and Environment, Agriculture, Road Safety, and Good Governance. The Board comprises both founding and appointed members. Its responsibilities include proposing candidates for vacant positions, reviewing contracts, making key organizational decisions, receiving and approving the budget, and providing guidance to the management unit on implementation and oversight.
